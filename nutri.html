Dietary Information





Nutrients in Paneer Butter Masala

1. Calories:

~400-450 kcal

2. Macronutrients:

Protein: ~12-15g (from paneer/cottage cheese)

Carbohydrates: ~20-25g (from the creamy sauce and base ingredients)

Fats: ~30-35g (from butter, cream, and paneer)

3. Micronutrients:

Calcium: High (~200-300mg, mainly from paneer)

Vitamin A: Moderate (from tomatoes and butter)

Vitamin C: Low to moderate (from tomatoes)

Iron: Low (from spices and paneer)

Potassium: Moderate (from tomatoes and paneer)


Common Allergens in Paneer Butter Masala:

Dairy: Paneer, butter, and cream are all dairy products, making this dish unsuitable for those with lactose intolerance or milk allergies.

Tree Nuts: Cashews (if used) may trigger nut allergies.

Spices: Though rare, some people may be sensitive to spices used in the dish.
